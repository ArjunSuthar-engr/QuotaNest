# QuotaNest

QuotaNest tracks your AI bot accounts’ credit reset cycles with a live countdown + progress bar.

## Dark mode (quick toggle)

1. Highlight/select the **entire** `QuotaNest` text in the header.
2. Release the mouse: the theme switches.
3. Highlight/select `QuotaNest` fully again to switch back.

The choice is saved in your browser (localStorage).

## Notes for reference-only accounts

When **Track timer** is turned off in Add/Edit, the card hides the timer/progress bar and shows your **Optional Note** instead.
